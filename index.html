The Checklist

A few essentials to make sure you're ready for the sun. Let's get packing!

Essentials

  • Passport (6+ months validity)
  • Flight Tickets / Boarding Passes
  • Travel Insurance
  • Hotel Confirmation
  • USD Cash for tips

Clothing

  • Light Daywear
  • Smart-Casual Evening Outfits
  • Swimwear (at least 2-3)
  • Beach Cover-ups
  • Light Sweater or Jacket

Health

  • Prescription Medications
  • Basic First-Aid Kit
  • Insect Repellent (DEET)
  • After-sun Lotion
  • Reef-Safe Sunscreen
